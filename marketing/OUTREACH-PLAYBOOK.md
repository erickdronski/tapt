# Tapt — Partner Outreach Playbook

The repeatable path from cold venue → claimed profile → paying partner.
Voice: the knowledgeable friend at the bar (docs/06). Never spammy, never
desperate — we're handing them free tools and local drinkers.

## The sequence (per venue)
1. **Day 0 — Email first-touch** (template A)
2. **Day 3 — Instagram follow + genuine engagement** (comment on a real post
   — their release, their event; no copy-paste comments)
3. **Day 7 — IG DM** (template B, only after the follow)
4. **Day 10–14 — Visit in person** where local; log a pour at their bar,
   show them their venue already live on the Tapt map
5. **Close — Founding Partner offer** (template C) or a Tapt's Favorite visit
6. **No response after 3 touches → 60-day parking lot.** Never more than 3.

## Template A — first-touch email
Subject: `Your taproom is already on Tapt's map. Want the keys?`

> Hi {name},
>
> I'm Erick, building Tapt, THE Beer Superapp, free for drinkers.
> {Venue} is already on our provenance-backed beer map, and locals can log
> pours there today.
>
> Claiming your profile is free, forever: publish what's pouring, see real
> local activity, and show up when nearby beer fans open the map. No
> expensive subscription. Working tools are free because active taprooms are
> what make the app good.
>
> Want me to send the claim link? Two minutes, no catch.
>
> Cheers, Erick · tapt-landing-three.vercel.app · sent from a real person

## Template B — IG DM (after following + engaging)
> Hey! Local beer app builder here — {venue} is already on Tapt's map and I'd
> love to hand you the profile before we push the {metro} launch. Free
> forever for the working tools. Mind if I email the details? 🍺

## Template C — Founding Partner close
> You'd be one of our first ten {metro} partners, so: **Founding Partner**
> status — 6 months of Featured placement free, your logo on our site, and a
> shoutout in the launch issue of The Tapt Dispatch. After that it's $29/mo
> if you want to stay Featured — and $0 forever for everything else either
> way. In exchange: keep your taps current, and let us photograph one shelf
> visit for a "Tapt's Favorite" story if the beer earns it.

## Objection cards
- **"We're on Untappd."** Great — stay there. Tapt is free where they charge
  four figures, and we never bury you behind a paywall. Nothing to migrate;
  claiming takes two minutes.
- **"Who's on it?"** Honest answer pre-launch: "We're launching {metro} first
  with the densest beer map anywhere — you're early, which is exactly why the
  founding offer exists."
- **"What's the catch?"** Drinkers never pay; venues only pay to be louder.
  Placement is never ranked by money against non-payers — that promise is on
  our landing page.

## Escalation targets (after 25 venue claims)
- Local beer writers/podcasters (Tapt Voices intros — permissioned content)
- Metro brewers' guild (one email, many breweries)
- Distributor rep (Manhattan Beer-type; fleet-claim pilot, Wave 4)

## Tracking
`partner_inquiry` table catches inbound; outbound lives in a simple sheet
(venue, contact, touch dates, stage, notes) until volume justifies more.
Every claim, stamp, and paid conversion gets logged — the deck's traction
slide only ever shows these real numbers.
