# Tapt — Instagram Launch Calendar (first 30 days)

> Note: `LAUNCH-KIT.md` in this folder is the current, ready-to-post source (updated real numbers, honest TestFlight-phase CTA, the Beer Market + Dispatch, copy-paste captions). This file remains as the 30-day skeleton.


Handle to claim: **@taptbeer** (fallback @tapt.app). Bio: "THE Beer Superapp
🍺 Scan any beer on Earth · Stamp your Passport · Free forever" + landing link.

Rules: real app screens, real venues (with permission), vector brand art —
never stock photos of strangers drinking, never fabricated numbers, always
21+ gate language in bio, "Enjoy responsibly" in profile.

## Content pillars (rotate; 4 posts + 3 stories/week)
1. **Passport** — stamps, country grids, "where has your palate been?"
2. **Beer of the Week** — the live race, Monday winner crowns
3. **Beer School** — one real fact, beautifully typeset (history timeline is 19 deep)
4. **Venue spotlight** — Founding Partners, Tapt's Favorite visits
5. **Zero-proof** — NA picks as first-class (own the niche the big apps ignore)
6. **Game night** — Beer Olympics medal tables, guide teasers
