# Tapt — Instagram Launch Kit (ready to post)

Everything to start posting the moment the account exists. Extends
`LAUNCH-CALENDAR.md` with current real numbers, honest launch framing, the new
features (Beer Market, in-app brewery tools, the Dispatch), and copy-paste captions.

Standing rules (non-negotiable): real app screens + real venues (with permission)
+ vector brand art only. **No fabricated numbers** (no fake follower/user/vote
counts, ever). 21+ / legal-drinking-age language in bio. "Enjoy responsibly" in
profile. Clean modern captions (no em-dashes).

---

## 0. The honest launch reality (read first)
The app is on **TestFlight**, not yet on the public App Store. So do **not** post
"Download on the App Store" yet. The Phase-0 goal is to **build an audience and a
waitlist**, not chase installs that aren't purchasable.

- **Primary call to action until App Store approval:** "Join The Tapt Dispatch /
  get early access" -> the landing page + newsletter signup (both live).
- **Pivot the CTA to "Download free" the day the App Store listing goes live.**
- This keeps every post truthful and still builds real momentum (email list =
  your launch-day cannon).

Link in bio: `tapt-landing-three.vercel.app` (the newsletter tile is right there).

## 6. Production + cadence
- Batch-produce Sundays. The app renders most visuals (real screens + share cards);
  screenshot at 3x on device for crispness. Landing sections also make clean cards.
- Palette: Pour Gold `#F2A900` / Malt Black `#1A1206` / Foam `#FBF6EC`; Poppins
  headlines. Assets in `brand/` (see `brand/ASSETS.md`).
- Every venue photo needs explicit permission (a DM confirmation is enough).
- Reels > static for reach; post 2-3 reels/week (scan flow, beer-page tour, game night).
- Engage 15 min after every post (reply, follow back beer accounts, comment on
  local breweries). Early on, engagement > posting volume.
