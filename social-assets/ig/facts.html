DID YOU KNOW
Guinness pours
like it's going
backwards.
It's the nitrogen. Bubbles get pushed down the sides of the glass and rise up the middle, so the settle looks reversed.

BEER SCHOOL
Lager vs ale
isn't about
color.
It's the yeast. Ale ferments warm and fruity at the top. Lager ferments cold and clean at the bottom. Both can be pale or dark.
