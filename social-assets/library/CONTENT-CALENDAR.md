## Format mix
- **Single posts:** 18  ·  **Carousels:** 8 sets  ·  **Stories:** 4
- Launch run front-loaded on days 1 to 5.
- Pillars rotate: identity, the Beer Market, history, taste, trending, partners, games, seasonal, community.

## Story ideas (fast, native, reusable)
- This or that: IPA/Stout, Bottle/Can, Hoppy/Malty, Light/Dark
- Rate this beer 1 to 5 slider over a Taste & Feel frame
- Question sticker: "What are you drinking tonight?"
- Behind the build: one real app screen and one line
