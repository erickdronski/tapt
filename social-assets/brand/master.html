Voice

Plain. Confident. Never salesy.

Do

  • Lead with what it is: "The beer superapp."
  • Short verbs, concrete nouns. Then stop.
  • Showcase the free things, plainly.
  • Let the product speak. Confidence over pitch.

Don't

  • Flex "real data." It's assumed.
  • Flex numbers or stat counts.
  • Hype verbs (elevate, seamless, unleash).
  • Em dashes or "it's not X, it's Y."
