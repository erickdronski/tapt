Privacy policy

Effective and last updated: July 11, 2026

Tapt uses account and beer activity data to run your Cellar, Passport, social features, and local discovery. We do not use cross-app tracking, and optional aggregated market insights remain off unless you opt in.

1. Who can use Tapt

Tapt is for people who are of legal drinking age where they live. In the United States, that means 21 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone below the applicable legal drinking age.

2. Information we collect

  • Account and profile: email address, authentication provider, display name, handle, avatar selection, age-confirmation status, and preferences.
  • Beer activity: beers you scan, save, rate, vote on, or log; check-in time; flavor tags; private notes; follows; blocks; and reports.
  • Location: device location only after permission, to find nearby beer venues and regional content. A venue may be attached to a check-in when you choose one. You can disable location in iOS Settings.
  • Partner content: venue claims, tap lists, event details, and branding uploaded through Tapt for Business.
  • Technical records: IP address, request time, app version, and basic security or error records created by our hosting and authentication providers.

Tapt does not request your contacts, advertising identifier, health data, or payment-card information. The iOS app does not include a third-party advertising or cross-app tracking SDK.

3. How we use information

  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Build your Cellar, Passport, taste profile, votes, and social experience.
  • Show relevant beers, breweries, pubs, bars, taprooms, and beer gardens.
  • Operate partner menus, venue claims, moderation, customer support, and fraud prevention.
  • Improve reliability and understand aggregate product performance.

4. Aggregated insights and your choice

Tapt may create regional beer-demand statistics only from eligible, consented activity and only after privacy thresholds are met. Those statistics are aggregated and are not designed to identify an individual. The optional sale/share control is off by default. You can change it in You > Privacy & data.

We do not sell information that identifies you. We do not combine your activity with third-party data for targeted advertising.

5. When information is shared

  • Service providers: Supabase provides database, authentication, storage, and backend infrastructure; Vercel hosts Tapt web pages. They process data to provide those services.
  • Sign-in providers: Apple, Google, Facebook, or another provider processes information when you choose that sign-in method, under its own privacy policy.
  • Safety and law: we may preserve or disclose information when reasonably necessary to comply with law, investigate abuse, or protect people and the service.
  • Business changes: information may transfer as part of a merger, financing, acquisition, reorganization, or sale, subject to this policy or a policy that provides comparable notice and choices.

6. Your privacy choices

  • Access and correction: review and update profile and preference information in the app.
  • Location: change permission in iOS Settings at any time.
  • Sale/share and analytics choices: use the controls in You > Privacy & data. Tapt honors Global Privacy Control where required.
  • Account deletion: choose You > Delete account. After confirmation, Tapt deletes your account identity and personal activity from the live service. Privacy-preserving aggregate statistics that cannot reasonably identify you may remain.
  • Other rights: depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object, or receive a portable copy of personal information.

To make a privacy request outside the app, email esdronski@gmail.com. We may need to verify that the request belongs to you.

7. Retention and security

We keep personal information while your account is active and as needed to operate Tapt, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, prevent abuse, and meet legal obligations. We use transport encryption, access controls, database row-level security, and scoped storage permissions. No system can guarantee absolute security.

8. International processing

Tapt and its providers may process information in the United States and other countries. Where required, transfers use recognized contractual or legal safeguards.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as Tapt changes. We will publish the new date here and provide additional notice in the app when a material change requires it.

10. Contact

Questions or privacy requests: esdronski@gmail.com.
