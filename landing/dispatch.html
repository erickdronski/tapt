🧪 Style science

Belgian blondes get their pepper-and-clove lift from the yeast, not the hops. Belgian strains throw off spicy, fruity compounds when fermented warm, which is why a simple grain bill can taste this complex.

📖 A story worth pouring
The gnome in the Ardennes

Achouffe started in 1982 as two brothers-in-law brewing in a tiny Wallonian village. The fairy-tale gnome on every bottle came from local Ardennes folklore, and the brewery leaned all the way in, hosting a yearly gnome festival long before it was acquired and shipped worldwide.
