Get help with Tapt.

Include the app version from You > About Tapt, your device model, and what you expected to happen. Do not email passwords, one-time codes, or private authentication links.

Email Tapt support

Sign-in email did not arrive

Check spam, wait a few minutes, then request one new message. Use either the link or the six-digit code from the newest email.

Google sign-in is blocked

Use email sign-in while the provider completes authorization. Send the exact provider message and app version to support.

Delete your account

Open You, choose Delete account, and confirm. Tapt deletes the account identity and personal activity from the live service.

Correct beer or venue data

Email the beer or venue name, city, and source link. Tapt keeps corrections evidence-based and does not invent listings.

Claim a brewery, bar, or pub

Use Tapt for Business to sign in, find the venue, submit a claim, and publish a free live menu after approval.

Send TestFlight feedback

Take a screenshot in Tapt, tap the screenshot preview, then choose Share Beta Feedback. The option appears only for assigned testers whose TestFlight beta group has feedback enabled. Tapt's release workflow checks and enables that group setting after each processed build.
